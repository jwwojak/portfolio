    About¶

    Senior Technical Writer specializing in documentation for robotics and SaaS. I distill complex technical systems and services, including software platforms and electromechanical systems, into clear, accessible manuals. Expert in building end-to-end writing strategies using Markdown, Git, and MkDocs to deliver precise documentation for products ranging from lab automation to cloud-based services.

    • Developed the Flex Preventative Maintenance and Service Manual, a proprietary resource detailing circuit board specifications, electrical protocols, and complex hardware replacement procedures.
    • Co-authored the 150 page Flex Instruction Manual, a comprehensive online resource for the flagship liquid handling robotics platform.

    • Collaborated with product managers and engineering teams to verify technical accuracy and document system failure states.
